WORDLE

How To Play

Use your keyboard or click the on-screen keys to guess the hidden 5-letter word.

You have 6 attempts to find the correct word.

Each guess must be a real 5-letter word. Press "Enter" to submit your guess.

After submitting a guess, the tiles will change color to show how close you are:
